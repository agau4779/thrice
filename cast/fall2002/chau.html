Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
I was first introduced to TR through Reach. Having a genuine interest in drama, I thought TR would be a good forum for me because the people involved seemed to be very approachable as well as sincere and devoted to acting.

What did you expect to gain from TR?
Through TR I want to understand how people acquire acting techniques. I would like my experience to uncover the many levels of an actor’s mind and functions. I am hoping this forum will help me uncover the psychological and motivational factors a person has to drop inhibitions. I want to see how people let themselves go and feel while trying to control raw emotions.

Where do you see yourself in five years?
If all goes well, in five, I would hope to have graduated from Berkeley with a marketable degree and go to law school or work on a ethnographic mapping research project in Hue, Vietnam. If none of these things come up I am going to go back to my hometown of Pomona, get my credential and teach at Pomona High School.

Who are your role models?
...Chaucer and Indiana Jones come to mind, but in reality my role models include many, but most importantly my Mommy Dearest. Just knowing who she is and what she’s done makes me feel motivated to just look for happiness in life and do the best for myself to help my family and others if I can.

What makes you smile?
The simple things in life, the small gestures which exhibit consideration and care are just some of the things which make me smile. I love to see growth and beauty in human beings, in whatever shape or form.
