Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
I wanted to be work within the medium of theatre with fellow Asian Americans. I've done a good amount of theatre but not with too many Asian peers. It's two of my favorite "things" combined, I guess. Does that make sense?

What did you expect to gain from TR?
An opportunity to unleash creative juices in my brain and let them flow forth on to the table so that they can mix with the creativity of others. A chance to see theatre that includes experiences and emotions that I have gone through as a person, a female, a student, an Asian American.

What do you do after Theatre Rice meetings?
When i do attend meetings, i usually go to the Ghetto afterwards with other Ricers. Or go home and sign online to talk to Ricers. :) OR try to get some last minute reading before i pass out.

Who are your role models?
my mother

What are your favorite movies?
AHHH! so many! Waiting for Guffman, The Apartment, Les Enfants du Paradis (Children of Paradise), to name just a few.
