Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
My friend Joyce recommended that I come to a meeting, so I did. I was hesitant at first, so I left early...but I made up my mind to join 15 minutes later and ran back to sign up for an audition. It was worth it!

What did you expect to gain from TR?
I already gained a new sense of confidence in myself, new lessons about theatre and teamwork, and most importantly, many life-long friendships.

Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
I see myself working hard trying to be successful...hopefully in the entertainment industry! My dream is to help people by performing on film and tv and tell their unheard stories.

Who are your role models?
God, my parents & extended family from Guam/mainland, Brian & Bruce Chargualaf, Leslie Travis, Alicia Taylor, Aaliyah, Sarah Michelle Gellar and a few more peeps...

What are your favorite movies?
My favorite movies are Spider-man, The Matrix, Both American Pie Films, Big Daddy, Mr. Deeds, Lord of the Rings, and Signs.

What makes you smile?
I smile when I share special moments or go places to chill with people who I care about. Also, I'm happy when I bump into someone I haven't seen for a while or when I help someone out.... I really enjoy company because there are many times I feel alone.
