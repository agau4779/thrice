Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
I got involved with Theatre Rice because I wanted to continue my interest in the performance arts in college. I graduated high school, having done performed a few plays my senior year and I really knew that theatre was something I would like to further pursue. When I joined this group I found each meeting to be really fun and an excellent way to use up study time. I also like being on stage, which probably won't happen for the rest of my tenure in Rice, unfortunately.
