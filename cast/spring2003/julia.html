Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
Back in high school, my Armenian/Iranian best friend and i basically added the only color to the drama department. In the theater department here at Cal i found myself still asking, "Where the Asians at?" i was drawn to Theatre Rice because it was a place for Asian American kids like me who were interested in the performing arts.

What did you expect to gain from TR?
Some laughs, good times, fun shows. But i've gained so much more. (enter cheesy music) LOTS of laugh, LOTS of good times (and some bad), memorable shows, and many kool friends along the way . . .

Where do you see yourself in five years??
In New York, checking out the theatre scene there, maybe even enrolled in grad school. Or in Los Angeles (where you see a movie star on every street corner!), checking out the theatre scene there.

Who are your role models?
Strong, funny, intelligent, beautiful women who speak their minds: my mother, my sister (Viva la CHO!), Margaret Cho, and the poet Nikki Giovanni.

What makes you smile?
My family, inside jokes, songs that make me sing & dance, unexpected gifts, a glimpse of something beautiful in the world as you're headed somewhere in a hurry . . . oh, Craig David's video for "What's Your Flava." :)
