Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
Theatre Rice was something I encountered before I even came to CAL at Reach!'s Senior Weekend. I was so impressed to see Asian Americans doing drama and comedy that I could relate to. You don't see too many Asian Americans doing drama and actuallybeing good. It made me proud to see young people make a statement about society and the issues we face. I wanted to be a part of that group.

What did you expect to gain from TR?
I wanted to learn how to better express myself and become a more insightful perfromer.

Who are your role models?
My mother is my role model because she always knows what to do when it seems like there's nothing that can be done, she's a person of integrity and she tells you like it is.

I can identify the blatantly obvious.
