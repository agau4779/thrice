Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
I thought, "Hmm what is something you can't picture yourself ever doing because you are too chicken?" And then I said, "Acting on stage in front of hundreds of people while trying to be funny at the same time!" Followed by, "Theatre Rice, here I come."

What did you expect to gain from TR?
I was hoping to gain some more self-confidence (I think I'm pretty shy) and make a lot of friends. So far I'd say that I've definately gained both.

What do you do after Theatre Rice meetings?
On a good day I go to the Ghetto with other ricers and watch them eat. Otherwise, I just retire to the blue house where I do nothing but homework, chat, and download new episodes of Buffy.

What makes you smile?
The absence of schoolwork. Perhaps that's why people say I need to smile more often.

Where do you see yourself in five years?
In some architecture studio (grad school maybe?) at 4AM trying to either draw or build something.
