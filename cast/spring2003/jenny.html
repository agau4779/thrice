Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
I got involved with TR because I thought I had potential in acting, that maybe one day, I might pursue a career as an actress. TR was a way for me to explore the field of acting and gain some experience on the stage. I found out that there are much more to acting than just acting in Theatre Rice. There are people relations, working hard and knowing when to just kick back and having some fun. Although I realize now that my potential in becoming an actress is not going to be surfacing in this life =P, I am very humbled by the fellow theatre ricers who puts so much on the stage.

What did you expect to gain from TR?
See above.

Where do you see yourself in five years??
In five years, I will be a budding neurosurgeon. The thought of being a neurosurgeon will force me to cherish the moments while I'm still in Theatre Rice.

Who are your role models?
Almost everyone I meet becomes my role model in one-way or another. I don't want to name names in theatre rice, I might make them feel embarrassed but they're there.
