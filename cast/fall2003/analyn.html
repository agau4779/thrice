Why did you get involved with Theatre Rice?
I love self _expression! And since I can't sing, write GOOD poetry or make anything with my two hands, I find myself deeply involved in displaying my many idiocentric personalities.

What do you hope to gain from TR?
More than anything, I really want to be a part of a very positive and close - knit Asian American community.

Who are your role models?
There are so many people that I admire and only one person who really inspires me in everything I do, and that's my mama. She is just a strong, amazing and beautiful pinay that cannot be compared to anyone (corny - I know).

What do you like to do in your spare time?
In my spare time I love to PAArty (Hang out with my PAAmily), eat my mom's cooking, and sleep.
