Theatre Rice T-Shirt Design Contest

The Theatre Rice T-Shirt is designed once every year and sold to members as well as the public (during shows). Take advantage of this great chance to showcase your artistic ability!

Requirements for Theatre Rice T-Shirt Design:
- Needs to have "Theatre Rice" on the design
- Background color should not be olive green, red, or grey (previously used colors)
- Artist should choose a color scheme (there can only be two colors total)
- Clarify which design is front and which is back
- Designs on Adobe Photoshop is preferred, but not required.
- Designs should be on A4 paper (It will be scanned and size-adjusted by company.)

Deadline
- Designs should be submitted by 9:30pm September 28 (Thursday) to any Committee member.

Contest Winner Gets...
Pride and accolades from all of us, but no cash or other forms of compensation.
